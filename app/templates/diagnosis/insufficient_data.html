How Diagnosis Works

Our analysis uses temporal lag windows to identify ingredients that frequently appear before symptoms occur. We analyze:

  • Immediate reactions (0-2 hours after eating)
  • Delayed reactions (4-24 hours)
  • Cumulative effects (24+ hours)

Results are grounded in medical research and include citations from reputable sources.
